Small facts about Ottaman empire

Ottaman empire is a powerful and ancient civilization.
Ottaman empire was a major political and military power in the medieval and early modern periods.
It was founded in 1299 and lasted until 1922.
The empire was known for its military strength, administrative efficiency, and cultural achievements.
